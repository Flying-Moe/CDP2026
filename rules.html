Welcome to the Cup of Death!

This game is played for fun. No vigilante work on the side.
The entrance fee is 15 €, and we are also playing for this grand trophy:

First Blood
First confirmed death of the season.

  1. Each contestant shall submit a list containing 20 celebrity names they think will die before the end of 2026. You must submit their full names by December 29th, 2025 at the latest. Submissions will be sent to: hreskesenlt@hotmail.com
  2. A celebrity, for the purpose of the dead pool, is defined as any person who is widely known. Should there be doubt whether or not the person is a celebrity, the final decision lies with the Grim Reaper (Game Master). A death must be confirmed in at least two trustworthy news media before it is accepted.
  3. The point system:
    Points will be awarded for each celebrity who dies in 2026. The points awarded will be calculated by the person's age at the time of their death. The calculation is as follows: 100 − age = points. Therefore, the younger your pick, the more potential points (example: Liam from One Direction died at the age of 31; 100 − 31 = 69 points).
  4. Any celebrity aged 99 and over is worth 1 point. Ages will be based on the published age at the time of death, as reported by the media.
  5. The burden of proof is upon the contestants. Notify the Grim Reaper when one of your celebrities has bitten the dust.
  6. Should one of your celebrity picks die during the game, you have 7 days from the time of death to make a new pick for your list and send it to the Grim Reaper. After 7 days, the right to pick fresh meat for your list expires.
  7. Start Date: This Dead Pool officially begins on January 1st, 2026.
  8. Any questions, conflicts, problems, decisions, or arbitrary assessments will be made by the Grim Reaper’s office, and their decisions are final. Whining or other forms of complaining will result in -1 point.
  9. July is sweep month. This means that you can switch your whole death list for an additional 15 euro. Your whole list must be brand new. No copies from your first picks.
  10. The scores and standings will be sent out at least once a month.

Winning Categories

  • First Blood – The player who predicts the first death of the game will win the before-mentioned grand trophy, which is theirs to keep and cherish for the next year. It will then be passed on to the winner of First Blood 2027.
  • Most overall points – The player with the highest total points by the end of 2026 wins everything from the prize pot.
