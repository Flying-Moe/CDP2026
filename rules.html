Rules – Celebrity Death Pool 2026

Home | Lists | Leaderboard | Statistics
  1. Each participant submits a list of 20 celebrities.
  2. A celebrity must be widely known.
  3. A death must be confirmed by at least two reliable news sources.
  4. Points are calculated as: 100 − age at death (minimum 1 point).
  5. If a pick dies, the player has 7 days to submit a replacement.
  6. The game starts on January 1st, 2026.
  7. July is sweep month. For an additional fee, a full list replacement is allowed.
  8. All final decisions are made by the Grim Reaper.
