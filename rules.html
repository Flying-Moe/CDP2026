Celebrity Dead Pool – Rules 2026

Home | Lists | Leaderboard | Statistics | Rules | Grim Reaper

Welcome to the Cup of Death. This game is played for fun. No vigilante work of any kind.

  1. Each participant must submit a list of 20 celebrities they believe will die during the year 2026. Lists must be submitted no later than December 29th, 2025.
  2. A celebrity is defined as a person who is widely known. In case of doubt, the final decision lies with the Grim Reaper.
  3. A death is only considered valid when confirmed by at least two trustworthy news sources.
  4. Points are awarded based on the age of the celebrity at the time of death, calculated as:
    100 − age = points
    Any celebrity aged 99 or older is worth a minimum of 1 point.
  5. When one of your picks dies, you have 7 days from the time of death to submit a replacement. After 7 days, the right to replace that pick expires.
  6. The game officially starts on January 1st, 2026. Deaths before this date do not count.
  7. July is sweep month. For an additional fee of 15€, a player may replace their entire list. The new list must be completely original and contain no names from the original list.
  8. Scores and standings will be published at least once per month.
  9. The Grim Reaper’s office has the final say in all matters. Complaining, whining, or other forms of poor sportsmanship may result in a −1 point penalty.

Winning Categories

  • First Blood – The player who correctly predicts the first confirmed death of the year wins the Grand Trophy, which is held until the following year.
  • Most Total Points – The player with the highest total score at the end of 2026 wins the entire prize pool.
